Featured Thesis

400,000 TPY PTA
Purification Plant

Designed a parallel PTA purification plant for Lotte Chemicals Pakistan Ltd., processing an additional 1,095 tonnes of Crude Terephthalic Acid per day to resolve a production bottleneck.

The design spans a packed-bed reactor with palladium/carbon catalyst, five flash crystallisers, a centrifuge, and a steam-tube rotary dryer — achieving 99.99% purity from a starting 99.7%.

Reactor and crystalliser units were modelled in Aspen HYSYS and Aspen Plus. Heat exchanger geometry was validated with Aspen EDR. A full HAZOP study was conducted on the reactor node.

The economic analysis projects a payback period of 2.9 years at a product price of $1,250/tonne.
